Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to an inclusive experience for every user.

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

1. Our Commitment

TRPSH — The Right Path Starts Here is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people of all abilities, including those with disabilities. We believe that every student, parent, mentor, and educator deserves equal access to the tools and resources that help prepare the next generation for the real world.

We are continuously working to improve the accessibility and usability of our website to ensure the best possible experience for all of our visitors.

2. Conformance Standard

We are working toward conformance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, language, learning, and neurological disabilities.

While we strive to meet Level AA conformance across all pages, we recognize that accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we are committed to continuous improvement.

3. Current Accessibility Features

Our website currently includes the following accessibility features:

  • Keyboard navigation — All interactive elements, including navigation menus, links, and buttons, are accessible via keyboard
  • Semantic HTML — We use proper heading hierarchy, landmarks, and semantic elements to provide a clear document structure for assistive technologies
  • Sufficient color contrast — Text and interactive elements maintain adequate contrast ratios against their backgrounds to ensure readability
  • Screen reader compatibility — We use ARIA labels, alt text for images, and descriptive link text to support screen readers and other assistive technologies
  • Responsive design — Our website adapts to different screen sizes and orientations, ensuring usability on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices
  • Focus indicators — Visible focus states are provided for interactive elements to support keyboard-only navigation
  • Readable typography — We use clear, legible fonts with appropriate sizing and line spacing for comfortable reading

4. Known Limitations

Despite our best efforts, some areas of the website may not yet be fully accessible. Known limitations include:

  • Some older content may not yet meet all WCAG 2.1 Level AA criteria
  • Certain interactive quiz elements may have limited screen reader support as we continue to refine them
  • Some third-party embedded content may not fully conform to our accessibility standards

We are actively working to identify and resolve these issues. As we discover accessibility gaps, we prioritize fixes in our development process.

5. Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is not a one-time effort — it is an ongoing commitment. We regularly review and test our website to identify opportunities for improvement. Our accessibility efforts include:

  • Regular manual and automated accessibility testing
  • Reviewing new content and features for accessibility before publication
  • Incorporating accessibility best practices into our design and development workflows
  • Staying current with evolving accessibility standards and guidelines

6. Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of the TRPSH website. If you encounter any accessibility barriers, have difficulty using any part of the site, or have suggestions for improvement, please let us know. Your input helps us serve all users better.

When reporting an accessibility issue, please include:

  • A description of the problem you encountered
  • The page URL where the issue occurred
  • The browser and assistive technology you were using (if applicable)
  • Any steps to reproduce the issue

We will do our best to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days and to resolve reported issues as quickly as possible.

7. Third-Party Content

Our website may include content or functionality provided by third-party services (such as embedded forms, analytics, or external links). While we encourage our partners to follow accessibility best practices, we cannot guarantee full accessibility of third-party content. If you encounter accessibility issues with third-party elements on our site, please contact us so we can work toward a solution.

8. Contact Us

If you have accessibility concerns or need assistance accessing any content on our website, please reach out:

TRPSH — The Right Path Starts Here
Email: contact@trpsh.com

We are here to help and are committed to providing an accessible experience for everyone.
