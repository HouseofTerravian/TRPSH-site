Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

TRPSH is designed for high school students ages 14–18, though the content is relevant for anyone ages 16–24 who is figuring out their next steps. The program is brought to schools through guidance counselors and administrators, and is also available for individual student enrollment.

The course is 100% online and self-paced. Students watch short video modules — averaging about 10 minutes per day — covering topics from career discovery to financial literacy to study habits. They can go at their own speed and complete it in 30 days or at a pace that works for them.

Schedule a free 20-minute demo call with our team. We'll walk you through the curriculum, explain school licensing options, and answer any questions. Most schools get up and running within two weeks of approval.

Yes. "What I Wish I Knew Before College" is a free digital download. No credit card required. We send it directly to your email. It's our way of starting the conversation and putting students on the right path from day one.

TRPSH partners with banks and credit unions to offer student-friendly accounts, secured credit cards, and financial literacy tools. Students enrolled in the TRPSH program can open accounts with partner institutions and begin building real financial history. Contact us for current partner availability in your area.

Absolutely. TRPSH is built for every student — whether they're headed to a four-year university, trade school, the military, entrepreneurship, or the workforce directly. The course dedicates full modules to non-college paths, including high-paying careers that don't require a degree.
